Peaky Blinders

Star Rating

8.8/10

Summary: The history of the group "Peaky Blinders" takes place after the First World War (1919) in Birmingham, England. The story continues the life and story of the group and in particular of its leader - Thomas Shelby. The group, which at the time was mainly interested in bookmaking, attracted the attention of Major Chester Campbell, a member of the Royal Irish Constance. Sent to Birmingham by order of Winston Churchill, he was tasked with "cleansing" the city of supporters of the Irish Republican Army, Communists and groups. Later, Thomas and his family face surprising events that turn their lives upside down.
