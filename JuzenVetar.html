South Wind/ Juzni Vetar

Star Rating

Summary: In the life of Petar Maras, a young member of the Belgrade car mafia, things began to take on a new meaning. He left his parents' home and moved to his new apartment with his longtime girlfriend Sofia. As a member of the clan ruled by an experienced "King", everything is going well. The work is done, the money is enough for everyone and the police do not create problems for them. Everything changed when Marash accidentally stole the wrong car. Soon Marash came into direct conflict with the leader of the second clan - Pigeon.
